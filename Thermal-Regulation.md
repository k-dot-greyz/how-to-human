### **thermal_regulation.md**

*Scripts for managing the system's thermal state.*

#### **1. force_coolant_reroute.sh (Hot Foot Soak)**

* **Purpose:** To address a common hardware bug where the system's coolant (blood) is poorly distributed, leading to cold peripherals (feet) and potential overclocking of the CPU (anxious mind). This script forces a thermal regulation cycle, drawing resources away from the overactive CPU and calming the system.  
* **Execution:**  
  1. Acquire a basin or container large enough for both foot peripherals.  
  2. Fill with hot water. The temperature should be hot but not scalding—think of it as a stress test, not a meltdown.  
  3. Submerge feet into the water.  
  4. The system will automatically begin rerouting thermal resources to the submerged peripherals. This often has a cascading effect, lowering CPU load and reducing system-wide anxiety.  
  5. Maintain for 10-15 minutes. Optional: Add Epsom salts (magnesium sulfate) to the water to patch potential magnesium deficiencies.
